# 🎯 Mock Interview - Quick Start Guide

## What You Can Do Now

### 1. Access Mock Interview (After Login)
- Look for **"Mock Interview"** in the navbar
- Only visible to logged-in users

### 2. Three Easy Steps

#### Step 1: Setup (2 minutes)
1. Select interview type:
   - 💻 **Technical** - Coding, system design, technical skills
   - 🗣️ **Behavioral** - Past experiences, soft skills
   - 📊 **Case Study** - Problem-solving, analytics
   - 👔 **HR Round** - Career goals, company fit
   - 🎯 **Mixed** - Combination of all types

2. Enter job details:
   - Job role (e.g., "Senior Software Engineer")
   - Experience level (Entry/Mid/Senior)
   - Full job description (paste from job posting)

3. Click **"Generate Interview Questions"**
   - AI creates 5-10 personalized questions
   - Takes ~10 seconds

#### Step 2: Take Interview (15-20 minutes)
1. Click **"Start Interview"**
2. Enable fullscreen (recommended)
3. Listen to questions (auto-read with voice)
4. Type your answers
5. Navigate freely between questions
6. Submit when complete

#### Step 3: Get Results (Instant)
- Overall score (0-100)
- Performance level
- Key strengths
- Areas to improve
- Question-by-question feedback
- Recommended next steps

## 🎓 Scoring Guide

### What Each Score Means

**Individual Questions (0-10)**
- **9-10** 🌟 Excellent - Perfect answer
- **7-8** ✅ Good - Solid answer
- **5-6** ⚠️ Average - Needs more detail
- **3-4** ❌ Below Average - Weak answer
- **0-2** 🚫 Poor - Inadequate

**Overall Score (0-100)**
- **90-100** 🏆 Excellent - Interview Ready!
- **75-89** 🎯 Good - Minor improvements needed
- **60-74** 📚 Average - More practice recommended
- **Below 60** 🎓 Needs Work - Study and retry

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Issue: Voice not working**
- Check browser permissions (allow audio)
- Try Chrome/Edge (best support)
- Click voice toggle off/on

**Issue: Fullscreen not working**
- Press F11 or click maximize icon
- Check browser fullscreen permissions
- Use Escape to exit fullscreen

**Issue: Questions not generating**
- Check internet connection
- Wait up to 30 seconds
- Try shorter job description
- Refresh and try again

**Issue: Can't submit interview**
- Check if any validation errors
- Ensure at least 1 answer provided
- Try "Submit" button on last question
