## How to Use

### Step 1: Generate Your Learning Path

1. Go to the **Learning Path** page
2. Fill out the form:
   - **Current Skills** (optional): What you already know
   - **Target Role** (required): Your dream job (e.g., "Full Stack Developer")
   - **Learning Goal** (required): What you want to achieve
   - **Time Commitment**: How many hours per week you can dedicate

3. Click **"Generate My Learning Path"**
4. Wait a few seconds while AI creates your personalized path

### Step 2: Explore Your Learning Journey

Once generated, you'll see:

```

│  🎯 Your Learning Journey                   │
│  ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━   │
│  Overall Progress: 20% (2/10 steps)         │

         ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐

│  Step 1 - HTML & CSS Fundamentals           │
│  ⭐ Foundation Phase                        │
│  ⏰ 2-3 weeks  🎯 Beginner                  │
│                                              │

         ↓

│  Step 2 - JavaScript Essentials             │
│  🔒 Locked (Complete previous step)         │
```

### Step 3: Navigate Through Steps

**Expand a Step:**
- Click the **▼** button to see full details
- View comprehensive descriptions
- Access learning resources
- See expected outcomes

**Active Step Features:**
- ⚡ Lightning bolt icon
- Pulsing glow effect
- Floating avatar animation
- Highlighted border

**Mark as Completed:**
- Click **"Mark as Completed"** button
- Step turns green with ✅ checkmark
- Progress bar updates
- Next step unlocks automatically

**Navigate:**
- Use floating **◀ ▶** buttons at bottom right
- Or click "Next Step" inside expanded cards
- Auto-scrolls to active step

### Step 4: Track Your Progress

**Progress Bar (Top):**
- Shows completion percentage
- Displays completed vs remaining steps
- Animated shimmer effect

**Step Status Colors:**
- 🔵 **Blue/Purple**: Active current step
- ✅ **Green**: Completed step
- ⚪ **Gray**: Future locked step

### Step 5: Complete Your Journey

When you finish the last step:
- 🎉 **Celebration animation** appears
- 🏆 Trophy icon with confetti
- Congratulations message
- Option to continue or start new path

## Visual Elements Explained

### Avatar Meanings

Each step has a unique avatar that represents your progress level:

1. **Beginner** (Steps 1-2): Blue background avatar - just starting out
2. **Learning** (Steps 3-4): Purple background - actively learning
3. **Intermediate** (Steps 5-6): Pink background - gaining confidence
4. **Advanced** (Steps 7-8): Green background - mastering concepts
5. **Expert** (Steps 9-10): Yellow background - almost there!
6. **Master** (Final steps): Red background - journey complete!

### Animations You'll See

1. **Float Effect**: Avatars gently float up and down
2. **Pulse Effect**: Active step has a subtle pulsing glow
3. **Shimmer**: Progress bar has a moving shine
4. **Glow**: Connection lines between steps glow rhythmically
5. **Slide Down**: Content smoothly expands when you click
6. **Bounce In**: Celebration popup bounces in playfully
7. **Fade In**: Smooth appearance of new elements

### Icons & Their Meanings

- 🚀 **Rocket**: Your learning journey header
- ⚡ **Lightning**: Active current step
- 🏆 **Trophy**: Completed achievements
- 🧠 **Brain**: Unlocked but not started
- ⭐ **Star**: Phase badges

- ⏰ **Clock**: Time estimates
- 📚 **Book**: Learning content
- 📈 **Trending Up**: Expected outcomes
- 💻 **Code**: Resources section
- ▶️ **Play**: Resource links

## Pro Tips for Best Experience

### 1. **Follow the Order**
Don't skip steps! Each builds on the previous one. The lock system prevents jumping ahead.

### 2. **Expand Every Step**
Read the full descriptions and outcomes. They provide context and motivation.

### 3. **Use the Resources**
Click on all the resource links. They're curated specifically for your learning path.

### 4. **Mark Honestly**
Only mark steps as completed when you truly understand the material. This helps track real progress.

### 5. **Take Breaks**
The time estimates are guidelines. Go at your own pace and don't burn out.

### 6. **Review the Tips**
Scroll to the bottom for success tips tailored to your journey.

### 7. **Save Your Paths**
Your progress is automatically saved. Check the left sidebar for all saved paths.

## Troubleshooting

### Avatars Not Showing?
**Solution:** Check your internet connection. Avatars load from an external API.

### Animations Laggy?
**Solution:** Close other browser tabs. Try a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).

### Can't Mark as Completed?
**Solution:** Complete previous steps first. The system locks future steps.

### Progress Not Saving?
**Solution:** Make sure you're logged in. Check browser console for errors.

### Steps Not Expanding?
**Solution:** Click the ▼ button on the right side of each step card.
